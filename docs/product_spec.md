# CyberConfessional V3.0 Product Spec (The Subterranean Hope)
**Based on: docs/PRD.md & User Feedback**
**Date: 2026-03-30**

## 1. Core Philosophy (The Odyssey Period -> True Love Waits)
*   **Aesthetic & Tone**: The product transitions from the cold, deterministic, and glitchy "digital asylum" of *OK Computer* into the humanist catharsis of *Let Down* and *Street Spirit*.
*   **Vibe**: We must show that the coldness of causality is not meant to punish, but to liberate. By realizing that things are deterministic, the user is freed from the burden of regret. "In math we trust, in causality we find peace."

## 2. Feature Breakdown
1.  **AI Prompt Overhaul (Cathartic Phase)**:
    *   **The Pivot**: After delivering the cold diagnostic of $U$ (Hidden Variables) and $M$ (Self-destruction), the `GENERATE_VERDICT.j2` prompt must inject a third, profoundly hopeful phase. 
    *   **Humanist Core**: Inform the user: *Because the past was inevitable (structural), regret is a mathematical fallacy. Your suffering is just the necessary algorithm forging your current self. In this realization, you are free.*
    *   **Radiohead Lyrics Injection**: Incorporate quotes like "One day I am gonna grow wings, a chemical reaction" (*Let Down*), "I'm not living, I'm just killing time... but true love waits" (*True Love Waits*), and "Immerse your soul in love" (*Street Spirit*).
2.  **UI & Slogan Restoration**:
    *   **Footer Restoration**: Restore the slogan `> In math we trust, in causality we find peace.` beneath `Everything in its right place.`
    *   **Cathartic Visualization**: The final paragraph of the verdict (The Hope phase) should perhaps be visually distinct (e.g., stripping away the danger-red glitch and replacing it with a soft, warm, stable glow).
